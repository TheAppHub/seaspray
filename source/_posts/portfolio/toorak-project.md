Set in the prestigious suburb of Toorak, this custom-designed concrete pool was created to seamlessly integrate with the home’s architectural form. Featuring a striking curved design that mirrors the residence, the pool becomes a true focal point, visible from expansive interior windows and enhancing the connection between indoor and outdoor living.

## Project Overview

Fully tiled in Luna, a natural stone-look ceramic, the pool delivers a soft, coastal-inspired water colour that complements the home’s light palette. Positioned strategically within the front yard of a corner block, the design maximises usable space while maintaining a refined, open aesthetic.

Innovation sits at the core of this project, with a negative edge system, acrylic viewing window, and multiple heating solutions working together to elevate both functionality and visual impact.

## Results

The finished result is a highly considered, architectural pool that feels completely integrated with the home rather than added to it. The curved structure enhances the flow of the property, while the infinity edge and concealed balance tank create a clean, uninterrupted visual line.

The inclusion of the acrylic window transforms the pool into an immersive design feature, offering a unique connection between the basement and outdoor space. Combined with year-round heating and carefully planned construction timing, the project delivers both everyday usability and a standout lifestyle experience.
